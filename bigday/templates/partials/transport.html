Transportation

Parking

We don't recommend driving to the wedding, as there will be an open bar.

That said, there are two parking lots available if needed.

  • The main lot charges $10 (cash only) per car, and spaces are not reserved or guaranteed. However, the lot is secure, well-marked, lit, and closest to the front door.
  • The overflow parking lot at 17th and Lucas is free to park in, but is further away from the entrance.

Shuttle

Instead of driving, we recommend taking the shuttle that will be running between the Pear Tree Inn Airport hotel and the venue!

  • Arriving City Museum: 5:05p
  • Departing Pear Tree Inn Airport: 5:30p
  • Arriving City Museum: 5:50p
