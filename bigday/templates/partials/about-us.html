Our Story

A long time ago, in a galaxy far, far away.... on the planet of Lowell, located in the outer rim of the Massachusetts system, a boy named Dan was running an experiment. He wanted to learn how people trust droids when they behaved in certain ways, so he brought people in to his lab to drive his small droid back and forth down a hallway, again and again.

In that same galaxy, a girl named Vicki was taking classes at a university on this very same planet of Lowell. She decided to drive the rebellious droid, which did not do anything she wanted. Suddenly the droid stopped responding altogether, and the boy saw that it not only disobeyed its driver, but also its maker.

As the boy waited for some technicians to speak with the droid, the two started talking together about what might have gone wrong with the droid's motivator. Even though conversing with the driver of the droid was forbidden, the boy liked the look of the girl and liked her ideas about droids. And of course, the girl liked the boy's droids, and the idea that the two of them could have many adventures together.

From that spring onwards, that is exactly what the two of them did. They climbed mountains, descended canyons, explored forbidden places, drove all over the country, ran a half marathon, visited foreign nations, and rode many roller coasters together. The more time they spent in each others company, the more they realized that by working together they could become very powerful! And that is why Dan asked Vicki to marry him, and why Vicki said yes! Now they are looking forward to a long future full of many more adventures!
